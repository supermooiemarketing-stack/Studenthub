About Us

Passionate students empowering local businesses through creative social media solutions

Our Mission

StudentHub Media was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to help small and starting businesses in Groningen amplify their digital presence through high-quality, engaging social media content.

We understand the challenges that small businesses face in standing out in today's crowded digital landscape. That's why we combine the fresh perspectives and creative energy of students with professional-grade equipment and proven marketing strategies to deliver exceptional results.

As a student-run initiative from Rijksuniversiteit Groningen, we bring both academic knowledge and real-world passion to every project, making professional social media marketing accessible and affordable for businesses of all sizes.

Our Approach

How we create value for small businesses

Who We Serve

Small and starting businesses across Groningen in both B2B and B2C markets. We focus on companies that want to grow their digital presence but may not have the resources for a full-time marketing team.

What We Deliver

Professional social media content, strategic lead generation campaigns, and comprehensive brand awareness initiatives across Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and TikTok.

How We Work

Through monthly subscriptions that provide consistent, high-quality content creation. We offer personal assistance and maintain close relationships with every client to ensure their success.

Our Resources

Professional audio, video, and lighting equipment combined with premium editing software. But our greatest resource is our passionate, dedicated team.
